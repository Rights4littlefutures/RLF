Our Purpose

RLF is an e-commerce non-profit dedicated to selling sustainable merchandise to fund Indian charities that combat child labor. Every purchase you make directly contributes to protecting children's rights and providing them with better opportunities for education and a brighter future.

We believe that ethical consumerism can be a powerful force for change. By choosing our products, you're not just buying quality items - you're investing in the lives of vulnerable children across India.
